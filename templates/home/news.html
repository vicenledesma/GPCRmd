Second simulation round

After our first successful publication in Nature Methods, we are back to increase the data coverage of GPCRmd in a 2nd simulation round....Show all

After our first successful publication in Nature Methods, we are back to increase the data coverage of GPCRmd in a 2nd simulation round.
In this round, we will focus on simulating all remaining PDB IDs which correspond to GPCRs which are not in complex with G proteins, arrestins, or nanobodies. Note that GPCR-G protein/arrestin/nanobody complexes will be addressed in the 3rd simulation round starting in January 2021. Hide
